## Scene-by-Scene Capture Instructions

Follow the [`scene_storyboard.md`](scene_storyboard.md) and [`shot_list.md`](shot_list.md) for precise timing.

### Scene 1 — Hero Landing (00:00–00:08)
- Scroll to **top** of page
- Let aurora animation play for 3 seconds
- Slowly scroll down to reveal the hero stats (5 stat cards)
- **Pause 2 seconds** on the hero CTA buttons

### Scene 2 — Architecture Pipeline (00:08–00:20)
- Continue scrolling to **System Architecture** section
- Hover over each **arch-stage** card (shows lift effect)
- Hover over each **weight-card** (35%, 25%, 25%, 15%)
- **Pause** on the code block formula for 3 seconds

### Scene 3 — Candidate Funnel (00:20–00:35)
- Scroll to **Candidate Filtering Funnel**
- Let the **counter animations** run: 100K → 1K → 100 → 10
- **Pause 2 seconds** on each funnel step card

### Scene 4 — Gold Podium (00:35–00:50)
- Scroll to **Top 3 Finalists — The Podium** section
- **Hover over each podium card** (Gold/Silver/Bronze lift effects)
- Stay on this section for 8 seconds

### Scene 5 — Results Table (00:50–01:05)
- Scroll to the **Top 100 Candidates** table
- **Click "Top 10"** filter button to show top 10
- **Click on Rank #1 row** to expand the reasoning panel
- Show the expanded full reasoning text for 4 seconds

### Scene 6 — Declarations & Footer (01:05–01:20)
- Scroll to **Compliance & Reproducibility**
- Hover over each declaration card
- End on **Team & Project Resources** section
- Let footer come into view
