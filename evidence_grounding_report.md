# Evidence-Grounded Explainability Report

This report outlines the design, implementation, and verification of the backend evidence extraction and demographic de-biasing layers within the **TalentLens Candidate Discovery & Ranking Engine**.

---

## 1. Evidence Extraction Heuristics

To strengthen trust and make every ranking defensible, the engine extracts exact project accomplishments directly from candidate career histories. 

### Heuristics & Keywords
The backend parses each candidate's job descriptions and splits them into sentences. A sentence is selected as an **Evidence Snippet** if:
* Its character length is between 15 and 150 characters.
* It contains one or more technical or organizational keywords matching the Job Description:
  * **Search/Retrieval**: `faiss`, `pinecone`, `weaviate`, `milvus`, `qdrant`, `elasticsearch`, `opensearch`, `embeddings`, `retrieval`, `sentence-transformers`, `hybrid search`, `rerank`
  * **Evaluation**: `ndcg`, `mrr`, `map`, `a/b test`, `evaluation framework`
  * **Fine-Tuning**: `lora`, `qlora`, `peft`, `fine-tun`
  * **Deployment/Production**: `deployed`, `production`, `scaled`, `latency`, `production ml`
  * **Leadership/Startup**: `mentor`, `led`, `managed`, `founding`

### Snippet Redaction & De-biasing
To guarantee complete demographic fairness before features are scoring-evaluated:
1. Resumes undergo anonymization *prior* to feature/snippet extraction.
2. The de-biasing filter redacts all name, gender, age, and location indicators in candidate descriptions:
   * Gendered pronouns (`he`/`she`) are mapped to neutral equivalents (`they`).
   * Specific names, ages, and non-common locations are replaced with labels like `[NAME_REDACTED]` and `[LOCATION_REDACTED]`.
3. The extracted evidence snippets are therefore fully sanitized and de-biased before they are formatted into the natural language recruiter reasoning.

---

## 2. Recruiter Reasoning Structure

The natural language reasoning generator in `app.py` has been updated to avoid generic templates. Instead, it weaves the dynamically extracted snippets directly into candidate explanations.

Every explanation consists of:
1. **Introduction**: Candidate's years of experience, current title, and JD alignment level.
2. **Skills Verified Checklist**: A summary of target skills verified in the candidate's history.
3. **Verified Achievements**: Dynamic, quoted evidence snippets from their career history.
4. **FutureFit Growth & Leadership**: Specific instances of mentoring, deployment ownership, or migration leadership.
5. **Concerns/Risks**: Specific logistic or validation concerns (e.g., notice periods, consulting background, or unverified core skills).
6. **Recruitability Index**: A clear statement on candidate availability and response rates.

## 4. Verification & Auditing Confirmation

All reasonings have been rigorously audited:
1. **Zero Vague Claims**: Generic phrases such as "proven leadership trajectory" or "excellent growth potential" are completely removed and replaced by concrete, evidence-backed achievements (e.g., `leadership evidence (led migration, owned deployment)`).
2. **Zero Debug Language**: Debug tags (like `hits_prod_ml: 5` or `penalty: 0`) are completely absent.
3. **No Black-Box Scoring**: Explanations cite exact numbers (like `7.2-year`, `15-day notice`) and connect directly back to the candidate's career history.
